TripMe

My journey began in the capital, Ljubljana, where the Ljubljanica River winds through the old town. The cobbled streets were full of charm, and I easily got lost in the outdoor markets, where local vendors sold fresh flowers and artisan cheeses. What fascinated me the most was the tranquil atmosphere, with cafés lining the river, where people spent hours chatting, and the city seemed to be in harmony with nature.

The next day, I headed to Lake Bled, one of the most iconic landscapes in Slovenia. The lake, with its emerald-green waters and the island in the center, felt like a dream. I rented a traditional boat, called a pletna, and went to the island to visit the old church. Upon arriving, the view of Bled in the distance left me speechless, as if time had stopped there.

Advice

  • Pack for all types of weather
    Slovenia has diverse climates—cold in the mountains, mild in the cities, and warm on the coast. Bring layers and good shoes.
  • Explore beyond Ljubljana
    Visit Lake Bled, Triglav National Park, Piran, and the caves of Postojna or Škocjan for a full experience.
  • Use public transport or rent a car
    Trains and buses are reliable, but a car is best for exploring the countryside and mountains.
  • Learn basic Slovenian phrases
    Most people speak English, but greetings like "hvala" (thank you) are appreciated.
  • Try the local food
    Sample dishes like žganci, kranjska klobasa (Carniolan sausage), and Bled cream cake (kremšnita).
  • Carry some cash
    The Euro is used, and cards are widely accepted, but rural areas may prefer cash.
  • Respect the environment
    Slovenia is very eco-conscious. Keep trails clean and follow nature preservation rules.
  • Enjoy outdoor adventures
    Hiking, skiing, biking, and rafting are popular—Slovenia is perfect for nature lovers.
  • Get travel insurance
    It may be required and is highly recommended for adventure activities.
  • Don’t forget the vignette
    You need a toll sticker (vignette) to drive on highways. Buy it at gas stations or online.
